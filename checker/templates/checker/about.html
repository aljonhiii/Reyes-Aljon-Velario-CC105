Methodology &
Performance Metrics

This predictor utilizes a supervised machine learning approach, specifically a Random Forest Classifier, trained on synthesized behavioral patterns derived from a user base of 7,500 records.

To ensure objective analysis, the model intentionally excludes demographic data such as age or gender. Instead, it evaluates potential addiction risk exclusively through composite digital habits—including total usage intensity, sleep-to-screen ratios, application open frequency, and weekend usage spikes.
